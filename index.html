About

My name is Alfred Loo. I am a second year Computer Science student @ Multimedia University, Malaysia. Currently I am working as IT Intern @ IOI Group. My hometown is in Melaka, where I live with my grandmother, mother and brother. I am the youngest in my family. My mother used to work as hotel manager, but she had retired 3 years ago.

During high school, I am very interested in Mathematics and Physics, and Mathematics had been my ace subject since I was grade 5. I liked the feeling of satisfaction after solving problems very much. After some great effort, I had a good result for my SPM. Then, I started to wonder which course should I take. I had consulted with one of my teacher. My teacher had suggested me to take software engineering course, which I had very less knowledge about at that time. I quickly made a few research on the course, and decided to study at Multimedia University.

At the beginning of the journey, I was inexperienced. There are fundamentals to learn and skills to master. Fortunately, with the help of my friends, we become better by learning from each other. Programming, databases, operating systems, algorithms, etc., these are the terms you won't hear that much during high school (at least for me). After learning them, I had realized their importance in our daily life.

Since then, I keep improving my skills and knowledge. I had created some simple application, including console application, desktop application and web application. Among those, I am mostly interested in web and mobile application development. Web and mobile applications have rapidly become part of our life in recent years. My current focus is on education and health industry. These two industries are mostly in need of some innovation. I have then started to brainstorm some ideas to improve both industries.

Last but not least, my ultimate aim is to realize my ideas and help serve the society, who gives a lot to me, in years to come.
